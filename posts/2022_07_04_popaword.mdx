# How to Play

## Basic Controls

Use your keyboard to type as many words as fast as you can.

There will always be at least one six letter word.

Type in your word and hit enter. If you're stuck, hit space to shuffle the letters.

# Scoring

The bigger the word the more points you get.

6 letter - 400 pts

5 letter - 300 pts

4 letter - 200 pts

3 letter - 100 pts

Example: "ACORN" = 300 points.

# Hints

Just SPAM anything pronounceable.

# Earn Bonus Points

Discover secret words that aren't in the word list and earn bonus points!
